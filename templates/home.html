What is UEBA?

User and Entity Behaviour Analytics (UEBA) is a cutting-edge cybersecurity solution that maps and analyzes user activity patterns over time. By establishing a baseline of normal behavior, the AI engine can instantly detect anomalies, isolate insider threats, and flag suspicious system interactions before a breach occurs.
