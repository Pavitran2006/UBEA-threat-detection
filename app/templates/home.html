What is UEBA Security?

UEBA (User and Entity Behavior Analytics) is an advanced cybersecurity system that uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to analyze user activities and detect abnormal behavior patterns that may indicate insider threats or cyber attacks.

Intelligent models uncover unusual patterns to stop threats early.

Real-Time Threat Monitoring

User Risk Scoring

Geo Login Tracking

Threat Intelligence Alerts

Receive automated warnings when indicators of compromise are detected.
